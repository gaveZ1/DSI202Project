WHY ABBZAAB

Indirect Heat System

The frying process is temperature controlled by the Japanese advanced technology “Indirect Heat System”. Where the oil will pass through the pipe to exchange heat with steam this will be able to control the temperature precisely and evenly to make frying evenly cooked. It doesn't cause any problems for the oil. The noodles absorb approximately 16-17% of the oil, and new oil is automatically added to the pan every hour.
